Penguin

Penguins are a type of flightless birds that live exlusively in the Southern Hemisphere of the Globe.

There are several types of penguins such as:

  • Emperor Penguin
  • King Penguin
  • Galapagos Penguins
  • etc..

Description:

  • Body is adapted for swimming
  • Large Head,Short Neck and Elongated Body
  • Usually Colored Dark on the back and Bright White in the underside

Population:

  • Currently there are approximately 11,654,000 Penguins
  • Most prominent population is found in the continent of Antarctica
  • The Galapagos Penguins are unique as they are endangered and is found
    in the Galapagos Islands near the equator
